Social Interactions

Lesson Overview

In this lesson, you will learn and practice basic social skills and interactions. These skills are essential for building relationships, communicating effectively, and navigating social situations with confidence.

Key Points

  • Understanding personal space and boundaries
  • Making eye contact and reading facial expressions
  • Starting and maintaining conversations
  • Taking turns in conversations and activities
  • Showing empathy and understanding others' perspectives

Practice Exercise

Choose a social scenario from the list below and practice with a family member or friend. Focus on applying the skills you've learned in this lesson.

  • Meeting someone new at school or work
  • Asking for help in a store
  • Joining a group conversation during lunch
  • Sharing an interesting story with a friend

After practicing, reflect on what went well and what you could improve next time.
