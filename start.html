Communication Skills

Lesson Overview

In this lesson, you will learn techniques to improve both verbal and non-verbal communication. Effective communication is crucial for expressing your thoughts, understanding others, and building strong relationships.

Verbal Communication

  • Clear speech
  • Tone of voice
  • Word choice
  • Active listening

Non-Verbal Communication

  • Body language
  • Facial expressions
  • Eye contact
  • Gestures

Key Points

  • Understanding the importance of both verbal and non-verbal cues
  • Practicing active listening and responding appropriately
  • Using appropriate tone and volume in different situations
  • Interpreting and using body language effectively
  • Developing strategies for clear and concise expression of thoughts

Practice Exercise

Try these exercises to improve your communication skills:

  1. Mirror Practice: Stand in front of a mirror and practice expressing different emotions through your facial expressions and body language.
  2. Conversation Role-play: With a partner, practice having a conversation about your day. Focus on maintaining appropriate eye contact, using gestures, and actively listening.
  3. Tone of Voice: Say the phrase "I'm fine" using different tones to convey different emotions (e.g., happy, sad, angry, excited). Ask someone to guess the emotion.
  4. Describe an Object: Choose an everyday object and describe it to someone without naming it. Practice being clear and concise in your description.

After each exercise, reflect on what you found challenging and what you'd like to improve.
