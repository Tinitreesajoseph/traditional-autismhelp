Understanding Emotions

Lesson Overview

In this lesson, you will learn about recognizing and expressing different emotions. Understanding emotions is crucial for improving social interactions and communication skills.

Key Points

  • Identifying basic emotions (happiness, sadness, anger, fear)
  • Recognizing facial expressions and body language
  • Understanding the causes of different emotions
  • Learning appropriate ways to express emotions
